Archive

The Cambridge University Go Society has a strong history, the last 30 years of which has been kept intact. Compiled here are the results of tournaments run by the club dating back to the early 1990s. The club also used to run a newsletter called 'Tesuji', for which a large number of issues are still available here as PDFs. Additionally, a number of photos have been preserved, all of which are also kept here in our photo archive.
